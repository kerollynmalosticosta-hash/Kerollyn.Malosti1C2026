7. Accessories: The Power of Details

1. Chic and Elegant: Timeless Classic

Focus on structured pieces, tailored clothing, evening dresses, and refined accessories. Perfect for women who appreciate sophistication and elegance.

Reference Brands: Belle Boutique • Divina Dama • Elegance & Co. • La Mode Chic

3. Romantic and Delicate: Femininity at Its Finest

Lace, ruffles, floral prints, bows, pastel colors, and flowing silhouettes create a soft and dreamy aesthetic.

Reference Brands: Flor de Lis • Doce Vestir • Sonho Rosa • Encanto

2. Modern and Minimalist: Less is More

Clean lines, neutral tones, straight cuts, and premium fabrics define this effortless style. Inspired by the concept of quiet luxury for everyday life.

Reference Brands: Aura • Lina • Sintonia • Traço
